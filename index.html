Hello. My name is Sara Hechmi, and I am a current first year undergraduate student at the University of California, Riverside double-majoring in Neuroscience and Computer Science. A career in healthcare has been one of the main things that has caught my attention, and influenced who I continue to do my best to be moving forward.

Along with my typical high school curriculum, I was able to work towards graduating with three Associates degrees in natural, and behavioral sciences. My dedication, and commitment to my academics has not only allowed me to challenge myself, but also gain so much insight about my potential, and develop strong student habits that keep me ahead of my study material. I am an avid learner, dedicated to furthering my knowledge, and using my experiences to shape the kind of medical professional I aspire to be.

My hands-on experience with medical practices, and medicine in general is very limited because of my age, but I have tried my best to get as much exposure to the field in any way I could have, given my own circumstances; I've virtually shadowed several healthcare professionals through clinical rounds, diagnoses, and attended webinars/ conventions that taught topics related to the medical field as a whole. Participating in such activities has helped expand my knowledge/ understanding of a variety of diseases, treatments, and surgical procedures, as well as what I should expect as I advance towards an education, and career in healthcare.
